Davis's Big Beautiful Money Tracker App for iPhone

Track every shift. See your pay grow in real time.

Davis's Big Beautiful Money Tracker App helps hourly workers track live earnings, compare gross pay with estimated take-home, stay on schedule with reminders, and review completed shifts without giving up privacy. No invitation, employer approval, or app-specific account is required.

Why People Use It

Built for hourly work, not generic budgeting.

Live shift tracking

Start a shift with one tap and watch your earnings update while you work.

Gross and take-home views

Switch between raw earnings and an estimated net paycheck view based on your settings.

Flexible pay rules

Handle pay changes, night differential, and overtime rules without a spreadsheet.

History and projections

Review completed shifts, schedule future ones, and see where your current pay period is heading.

Widgets and Live Activities

Keep your current shift and pay snapshots visible on the Lock Screen and Home Screen.

Private by design

Your data stays local-first, with no required account, employer login, or payroll-provider connection.

Included

Everything you need to stay ahead of your next paycheck.

  • One-tap clock in and clock out
  • Schedule templates and reminder support
  • Shift history with edit, duplicate, and reschedule tools
  • Siri shortcuts for starting and ending shifts
  • Pay period totals, projected paycheck, and all-time earnings
  • No required account or employer affiliation

Who It's For

Public, personal, and built for hourly work.

Davis's Big Beautiful Money Tracker App is intended for the general public, specifically individual hourly workers who want a personal shift and earnings tracker on iPhone.

The app is not limited to a single employer, payroll provider, client, or organization, and it does not require sign-up before use.
