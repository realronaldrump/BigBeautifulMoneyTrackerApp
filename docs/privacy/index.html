Overview

How Davis's Big Beautiful Money Tracker App handles your information

Davis's Big Beautiful Money Tracker App is designed to help you track shifts and earnings on your iPhone. The app is built to work without requiring you to create an account.

Based on the current app implementation and privacy manifests, Davis's Big Beautiful Money Tracker App does not declare data collection for tracking or third-party advertising.

Information You Enter

Shift, pay, and schedule information

When you use Davis's Big Beautiful Money Tracker App, you may enter information such as your hourly pay, pay schedule, tax estimate settings, shift history, notes, reminders, and schedule templates.

This information is used to provide the app's core features, including live earnings tracking, projected pay, widgets, reminders, and on-device history.

Storage

Local-first data handling

Davis's Big Beautiful Money Tracker App is local-first. Your information is intended to remain on your device first, and the app does not require a remote account to function.

If you enable Apple-provided device backup or related system services, your app data may be included in those Apple-managed backups according to your device settings.

System Features

Notifications, widgets, Live Activities, and shortcuts

Davis's Big Beautiful Money Tracker App can use Apple system features such as local notifications, widgets, Live Activities, and Siri shortcuts to show shift and earnings information on your device.

These features depend on permissions and settings you control on your iPhone.

Tracking

No third-party tracking declared

Davis's Big Beautiful Money Tracker App's current privacy manifests state that tracking is disabled and no collected data types are declared.

Contact

Questions about privacy
