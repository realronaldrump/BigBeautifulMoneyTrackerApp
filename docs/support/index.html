Compatibility

Supported platform

Davis's Big Beautiful Money Tracker App is designed for iPhone and requires iOS 26.0 or later.

Widgets, reminders, Siri shortcuts, and Live Activities require the related iOS features to be enabled on your device.

Privacy

Your data stays local-first

Davis's Big Beautiful Money Tracker App is built to keep your shift and pay information on-device first. The app does not require an account to work.

If you choose system-level backup options, your data may be included in your normal Apple backup flow according to your device settings.

Common Questions

Quick answers

Why is take-home pay labeled an estimate?

Tax withholding varies by your filing choices, deductions, and paycheck details, so the app provides an estimate instead of claiming exact payroll numbers.

Can I change my hourly rate later?

Yes. You can add pay changes in Settings and keep a history of rate updates over time.

Can I schedule shifts in advance?

Yes. You can create future shifts, set up repeating templates, and sync reminders for upcoming work.

Do I need to create an account?

No. The app is designed to be useful immediately without requiring sign-up.
